AI Marketplace Hunter v0.4.2 — Gemini Upgrade

1. Stop the current bot:

   Ctrl+C

2. Extract this ZIP into a new folder.

3. Copy your private local files:

   .env
   marketplace.db
   facebook-profile/
   facebook-profile-kk/
   facebook-profile-kl/

4. Install:

   py -m pip install -r requirements.txt
   py -m playwright install chromium

5. Remove old OpenAI settings from .env and add:

   AI_ENABLED=true
   GEMINI_API_KEY=your_gemini_api_key_here
   GEMINI_MODEL=gemini-3.5-flash
   GEMINI_TIMEOUT_SECONDS=30
   AI_MAX_LISTINGS_PER_SEARCH=5
   AI_CONCURRENCY=2

6. Start:

   py app.py

7. Test in Telegram:

   /aistatus
   /search iPhone 15 Pro under RM3000 cheapest
   /analyze RTX 3070 under RM1200

The normal marketplace crawler works even when Gemini is disabled or
temporarily unavailable.
