AI Marketplace Hunter v0.4.2.5 Hotfix

Problem:
TypeError: MarketplaceAIAnalyzer.analyze_top()
takes 1 positional argument but 3 were given

Cause:
analyze_top() declares query and scored_listings as
keyword-only arguments, but handlers.py passed them
positionally.

Fixed calls:

await ai_analyzer.analyze_top(
    query=request.query,
    scored_listings=scored,
)

The automatic AI analysis after /search was fixed too.

Installation:
Replace handlers.py in your project,
or use the full corrected ZIP.

Restart:
Ctrl+C
py app.py
