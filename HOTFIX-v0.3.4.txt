INSTALL

Replace the files from this ZIP in your v0.3.3 project.

Stop and restart:

    Ctrl+C
    py app.py

Test:

    /search RTX 3070 under RM1200 cheapest

OLD SAVED RESULTS

If /current still shows old incorrect GTX 1660 records, remove and
recreate the watch:

    /remove WATCH_ID
    /watch RTX 3070 under RM1200
