AI Marketplace Hunter v0.3.6
Facebook Card Matching Fix

ROOT CAUSE

Facebook detail pages contain recommended listings. The previous parser could
select a recommended "MSI RTX 3070" title and reuse it for unrelated links such
as GTX 1660, RTX 3060, or GTX 1070.

The crawler also shared one URL duplicate set across Kota Kinabalu and Kuala
Lumpur. If Facebook returned overlapping results, the KK search could consume
the URLs and leave the KL section empty.

FIXES

1. The Facebook search-card title is now the trusted product title.
2. The card title must match the requested GPU family before the item page is
   opened.
3. Detail pages may improve posted time, but they cannot replace the title.
4. Duplicate URL tracking is reset separately for KK and KL.
5. Every result remains grouped under the region that was actually searched.
6. Carousell still uses sort_by=3 for recent-first results.

EXPECTED

Query:

    /search RTX 3070 under RM1200 cheapest

Facebook results must have an RTX 3070-family title on the search card.

The following will not pass:

- GTX 1660
- GTX 1070
- RTX 3060
- RTX 3080
- Generic title with no RTX 3070 model

INSTALL

1. Stop the bot:

       Ctrl+C

2. Extract this ZIP and replace the project files.

3. Start:

       py app.py

4. Test:

       /search RTX 3070 under RM1200 cheapest

OLD WATCH DATA

Old incorrect records remain in marketplace.db. For /current, remove and
recreate the affected watch:

    /remove WATCH_ID
    /watch RTX 3070 under RM1200
